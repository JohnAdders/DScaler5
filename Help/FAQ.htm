Introduction

What is DScaler 5?

DScaler 5 is planned to be an attempt to build a fully functional media player targetted at large screen displays and high quality images and sound. Currently is consists of two filters and nothing else.

How to I use/test the filters?

The filters should just work in any appilcation that plays back MPEG 2 files.

Why aren't there more questions?

The project has only just started.
